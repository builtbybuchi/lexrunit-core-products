### Aim 

A hospital management system that completely replaces all manual operations in the hospital

Major problems:
1. Paper based patient data management
2. Cost of operations
3. In efficiency in staff management
4. In efficiency in resource management leading to wastage and expiration. 
5. Lack of easy access to patients.
6. Unpredictable/incosistent income stream
7. Long time spent on consultation

Problems we can't solve:
1. The short staffing of hospitals.
2. The lack of electricity.
3. Lack of good internet access. 
4. Lack of infrastructure, in fact. 

Other non-tech problems:
> Training health professionals on the use of this technology

Priorities:
- All hospitals internal operations should be offline
- The cost of adoption should be Zero naira(The hospitals should not have any added cost to use the HMS)

*** Remember the goal is that when the doctors are in the hospital they need only one software, LexCare HMS ***
